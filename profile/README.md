## What We Build

We create software that puts expert knowledge into the hands of frontline workers — through **smart glasses, phones, tablets, and whatever screen is already in front of them**. No headsets required. No training manuals. Just the right information, at the right moment, in context.

Our thesis is simple: the form factor changes, but the need doesn't. Whether it's an AI assistant on Ray-Ban Meta glasses guiding a field technician, a spatial AR overlay showing a surgeon what's next, or a warehouse worker getting haptic cues through their wrist — we build the layer that turns expertise into action.

## Who We Are

FlowsXR was founded by **[Prasanth Sasikumar](https://prasanthsasikumar.com)**, a researcher and builder with 8+ years in immersive technology. Former Research Fellow at the **National University of Singapore** (NUS Augmented Human Lab), IEEE CS member, and organizer for IEEE VR, ISMAR, and the Meta-sponsored ICE Workshop. His work spans human-computer interaction, spatial computing, and AI — with the core belief that the best interface is the one you forget you're wearing.
